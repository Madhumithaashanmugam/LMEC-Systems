About Us

A P LMEC SYSTEMS was established in the year 2007 as a Partnership Firm to manufacture and assemble precision engineering components and assemblies. The company caters exclusively for defence and aerospace applications. Today the company is considered one among the elite in its field, thanks to the commitment and quality of the products we have delivered over the years.

The items manufactured by the firm have to meet the stringent quality requirements of the missile. A well trained quality team shall oversee the quality requirement of the items right from the raw material stage to in-process inspection & final delivery of the items. The company is a ISO-9001-2000 certified and equipped with full-fledged Quality Control Department.

The company is equipped with precision machining facilities like CNC Machining Centers, Turning Centers, Precision Lathes & Milling machines, EDM, Grinding, Press working, TIG Welding, Pressure Testing, Furnace etc., including CNC Winding, Magnetising equipment, Soldering & Assembly Stations etc.

The manpower available with the firm are qualified engineers and technicians, well trained and highly skilled and capable of undertaking design and manufacture of Fixtures and Gauges required for the company operations including Process Planning, CNC Programming, Tool setting, Trouble shooting & Metrology.
